The dataset used to created this model contains a totoal of 15 NBA sesons from year 2003 to 2018. This excludes 2012 as the palyoffs were cancled that year. Each seson 16 teams are slected to the playoffs based on performance, thus we have a dataset of size 240. Logistic regression was used to model the Dataset to undersatnd whcih variables had the most impact on the play off results. We gave each team a variable betweeen 3 and 0 based on thier performance in the play offs which is variable “Champ”. The teams that won the play offs recived 3, and a value of 2 to the teams that won the conference fianls. A value of 1 was assigned to the teams taht won that conference semifianls.All the other teams recived a value of 0. The training dataset was created using half of the orginal dataset which was randomly selected. After performing backwards elimnation and using our intution several variables were considered innsignificant and was dropped. We dropped variables such as final win loos percentage(F.W.L.) and (CSF.W.L.) as they offerd no signifiancant understaing as to what factors led the teams to win. From this model we can see that both eleite defense and offensive players are very influencial to a teams chances of winning the NBA playoff, however defensive players seems to play more of an important role then the offensive palyers.
